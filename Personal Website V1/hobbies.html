My favorite video game series are Final Fantasy, Resident Evil and WWE. Final Fantasy is such a compelling, addictive RPG (Role Playing Games) that is full of adventures. It's the reason why I have grown to like RPG. Resident Evil is an action-horror video game that isn't that even scary. Silent Hill is a lot scarier than Resident Evil. It revolves on the main protagonist(s) quest to solve the mysteries behind the story. Lastly, WWE games are a simulation to its show counterpart. You get to play as the wrestlers seen on TV and just simply beat up other wrestlers as you see fit. People complain of its outdated roster and lack of improvements as WWE games are made every year. Yet, it's one of those game series that people anticipate for because they expect an updated roster and new features to be added as well as remove redundant features seen on past installments of the series.
