"No Internet Is Such A Dull Day"

I.

The internet is the source of everything

Games, references, tutorials, just about anything

Search the web in a single click

No, it's not some sort of magic trick

II.

When the internet goes down

My face paints a frown

The day feels slow and boring

Without you, I am nothing

III.

Having an internet connection is everyone's happiness

No access to e-mails, news, social medias, is our loneliness

Sadly, this is today's generation

The would-be product of the next incarnation

IV.

No internet is such a dull day

I am bored and yawning, you may say

Internet, don't leave me like other girls do

We have known each other for 12 years, too
