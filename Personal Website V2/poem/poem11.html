"There Is No One Else"

I.

You brought me to this world unknown to me

You raised me with utmost passion, a will so free

In situations where it'll be fight or flight

You propelled our personalities to the greatest height

II.

On your special day, we want to give you the best

You are the greatest mother incomparable among the rest

Despite the challenges that stood before us

You made amends to keep everything just

III.

We've been through rise and fall

No matter what, you make the best call

Mother has reaped the fruits of her labor

Wishing good health and prosperity is my favor

IV.

Let me thank you for all the hard work you did

The problems we encountered, we shall get rid

I'm proud to have the best mother

Our needs fulfilled we didn't need to bother
