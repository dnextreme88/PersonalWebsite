"The Calling"

I.

We will rise from the ashes

As the war of roses clashes

Humanity is in a perilous situation

Neutralize the nemesis without hesitation

II.

My brethren, join my conquest

Conquer the evil and their finest

There is no retreat nor surrender

Crush the opposition so tender

III.

The wicked will be punished greatly

To an army with weapons so deadly

Leave no stone unturned

Casualties and their bodies burned

IV.

At last, we are inches closer to victory

No need for a trip to the refinery

Your forces are gone, you're left to defend

I'm sorry that this is how it'll end
