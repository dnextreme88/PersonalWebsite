"Wings"

I.

A common misconception about wings

Is that you have full control like the kings

Wings symbolize freedom for those who has none

Fly to far-flung places and be gone

II.

Such an awesome feeling to soar so high

Dreams are limitless and the end is nigh!

See the world's magnificent view

In a nutshell, it looks so new!

III.

People are in awe when they see you fly

As long as you land properly, not dry

Be free like you just got out of prison

Take a rest in roofs and treat it a garrison

IV.

Soar like you're in a haste

Time in air is never a waste

If only wings are possible

Then my dreams are plausible!
