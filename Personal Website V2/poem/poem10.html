"Sisig Lord"

I.

When I first tasted you

My life felt so anew

Since then, you always satisfy my appetite

I chow you so fast like an armalite

II.

I never get to eat you at home

How I'd wish I eat you alone in a dome

Got no regrets spending a dime

You're so luscious it's worth the time

III.

Sisig is such a very rare food

Never fails to lighten my mood

Your simplicity shines over the rest

One of the meals I tasted the best!

IV.

Let's cut to the chase, I prised you too long

I spoke whole-heartedly, I wasn't wrong

I thought of my lines concisely, I dig

Until next time, sisig
