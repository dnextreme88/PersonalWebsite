"Invisibility"

I.

Imagine being the unknown

People wonder for you aren't shown

There's no limit to the stuff you take

And tell yourself "It's a piece of cake!"

II.

You're hidden, worry no more

Make it a habit, not a chore

Loot all the stuff you want

At night's end, you won't rant

III.

Sneak to the most private places

Dressing rooms, comfort rooms, without traces

You have an ample amount of time

For everything is worth a dime

IV.

Have possession of something you've yearned

Just never get caught, that's what you learned

People will never notice a thing

As you reach for the item you cling
