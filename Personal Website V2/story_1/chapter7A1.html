Chapter 7A1: When The Price Is Right

You picked Vengeance Line. Your decision will affect how the characters will act towards you.

Raz drew his blade and killed his fellow comrades. Anguish and anger in him, he was done with SOLDIER. He has betrayed his men. He and Jeffrey immediately left the ruins as it begins to crumble.

"Raz, you did the right thing. SOLDIER is nothing!" Jeffrey remarked as they successfully exited the ruins.
"I... feel no remorse. Indeed, it was right." Raz in a change of heart.
"You will prove your allegiance soon but right now, we leave these ruins immediately." Jeffrey telling Raz that there are still unfinished businesses he must do.

At twilight, the moon shines with luminosity. Jeffrey was laughing with joy as Raz laughed with hesitation. He was guilty at the same time, he achieved his vengeance. They headed to Jeffrey's HQ. Jeffrey introduced Raz to his new group.

"My friends, I am here to introduce to you a new and fresh face to be added into our roster that will bring doom to the world! Meet Raz, a former SOLDIER leader now joining us after betraying his men during the encounter at Glassheim!" Jeffrey happily remarked as Raz shows up to his new allies." Jeffrey happily remarked as Raz shows up to his new allies.

He was introduced. Jeffrey was so excited. Raz shyly looks at them one-by-one. Fresh faces, indeed. A combination of monsters and humans, he really needs to adjust into this new environment. Jeffrey told him his obligations.

"You don't just simply join us for nothing. To prove your worth, you will join my trusted friends into a journey and destroy your former allies." Jeffrey making a new mission for Raz.
"Where would that be? I thought you had seen me fighting already?" Raz asked as he thinks Jeffrey was just being hesitant.
"That wasn't enough. You do more than that." Jeffrey replied.

Raz then went with his new allies, without telling him where they'll go. Eventually, they took a rest and will depart the next day.
